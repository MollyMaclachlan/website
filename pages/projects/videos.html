Overview

I have a YouTube channel. I use it for a variety of things, such as streaming, highlight videos, music, and really anything else that pops into my head. Its primary purpose, though, is for streaming video game playthroughs.
At the time of writing, I've streamed Elden Ring, Dark Souls III and Pharaoh: A New Era, and I'm currently streaming my first playthroughs of Subnautica and Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice. It's good fun.
Before moving to YouTube, I used to stream on Twitch. The game I mostly streamed there was Hollow Knight, and those VODs are available on my archive channel on YouTube.
