I enjoy video games and I am a programmer. These two things naturally combine to produce the result of me creating mods for some of my favourite video games. All of my mods are available at my Nexus profile, but this page also lists them for your convenience.

No Dismemberment or Finishers

No Dismemberment or Finishers is is a simple mod for The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt that does exactly what it says on the tin: it disables mid-combat dismemberment and the slow motion finisher kills at the end of combat. It also has a variant disabling only mid-combat dismemberment, and another disabling only finishers.
You can download versions of this mod for both the 1.3x patch and the next generation patch. The versions are on separate Nexus listings and links to both can be found below.

Grimrock Enemy Imports

The Grimrock Enemy Imports are a series of mods I made for Legend of Grimrock 2, a dungeon crawler puzzle game I absolutely adore. These mods import enemies from the first game that weren't implemented in the second to allow other modders to use them in their custom dungeons.
So far, I have imported the Goromorg, Ice Lizard, Scavenger, Skeleton Warrior, Snail, Tunnel Ogre and Warden. Links to all can be found below.
