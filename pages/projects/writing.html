I first became interested in writing during my first year of secondary school, aged 11. I already loved reading, so instead of paying attention to my classes, I began writing blurbs for non-existent books.
After some time, I decided I wanted to make those books existent, and in fourth year, with some advice and encouragement from a truly wonderful English teacher, I first began properly writing and threw myself into the hobby. It was also around then that I joined AllPoetry.com, where I have now published over 80 poems.
On the subpages linked above, you can find specific information about my poetry and prose writing, projects I'm undertaking, plans I have, and one or two excerpts. I hope you enjoy what you find.
