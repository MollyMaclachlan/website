The Sleepy Quill

Overview

The Sleepy Quill is a substack newsletter/blog I started for fiction and non-fiction writing. I created it because the writing sites I'm on aren't well-suited to standalone short stories or non-fiction posts.
Writing published on The Sleepy Quill can be anything from short stories to miscellaneous non-fiction ramblings to politics. It's not currently active, but I'm hoping it will be again in the future.
