Prose

Overview

Prose creative writing, especially fantasy and science fiction, is my absolute favourite thing to do. I've written several short stories that are available on the internet and I'm always working on too many books. I'll admit I do have a tendency to get distracted worldbuilding and do too much of that instead of writing the actual story.
Below you can find information about and links to prose writing I've done that's available on the internet.

My accounts

I have accounts on several writing websites, including RoyalRoad and Scribble Hub, and a substack page called The Sleepy Quill, which you can read more about here.
Currently, these two accounts solely host Rooms of the Desolate, which is on hold for now. I aim to publish more writing here when I can just about find the time.
I do my best to synchronise releases on all of my accounts.

Rooms of the Desolate

  • RoyalRoad
  • Scribble Hub
  • The Sleepy Quill
Rooms of the Desolate is an ongoing collection of short stories written to guide the reader through the many rooms and mysteries of the bleak, greyscale labyrinth of the Desolate.
There are currently six entries in Rooms of the Desolate: The Forever Tower, Production Line, Bone Tithes, House of the Collector, Devils of the Deep, and The Wasteful Plain. Currently, writing of further stories is on hold, but I do have more planned for once I have time away from other projects.
