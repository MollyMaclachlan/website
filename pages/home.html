Welcome

Welcome my website! Here you'll find information about me, links to and embeds of various things I'm doing across the internet, and whatever other things pop in to my mind that I want to include here.

Web design isn't my strong suit, so if this website looks or functions a bit wierd in places, don't judge me too harshly. You can always use the contact link in the footer to e-mail me about it, and I'll do my best to fix it.

Bear in mind, also, that this site is still a work in progress. Some sections have barely any content, others perhaps a tad too much. I'm working to iron out the cinks and add more sections. I have a gallery page planned, as well as perhaps some more in-depth details of some of my most active accounts.

I've also got some backend plans in the works, such as moving some of the currently externally-hosted images to by internally-hosted, and moving over the scripts from JavaScript to TypeScript - for which I'll first need to learn TypeScript, of course!

About Me

I am Murdo Maclachlan. I'm an 18-year-old Scottish aspiring author and poet, currently studying for a Bachelor's degree in software engineering.

Despite my degree of choice, I intend to do everything I can to avoid working in computing. I have a great love of fantasy and science fiction and it's been my goal to write both professionally for over 7 years now. More detail on this matter can be found here.

There's probably stuff that can be said about me, but I've never been great, or even remotely good, at talking about myself, so I'll leave it instead at the fact that I've been described as Scottish Chewbacca. This was the best unintentional compliment I've ever received and remains the crowning moment of my life thus far.

Oh, one more thing! I can't of course be spoken about without mention of my wonderful cat, Curl. I intend to add a gallery page to this site at some point, which will most likely almost entirely consist of pictures of him.
