Accounts

Here you can find an alphabetical list of navigation links to my various internet accounts, accompanied by short explanations of what I generally do with them. I have a number of accounts I consider still in use, although activity may still be infrequent, but many more that are completely inactive. They are listed at the bottom of this page, though there are likely some I've forgotten.

AllPoetry

I'm on AllPoetry, a large poetry-sharing website, as Murdo Maclachlan.

This is one of my least active "officially" active accounts at the moment, as I'm focusing much more on prose writing than poetry. I still come back and write a poem every now and then though.

DeviantArt

Another very inactive "active" account, I joined DeviantArt several years ago, as MBMaclachlan, but have only uploaded a few sketches, none of which are very good.

The image to the left is a great helm sketch I published in May 2020. It's one of my favourites among the images I published to DeviantArt, along side the bastard sword sketch I published the same day.

Discord

I use Discord for a lot of my communication with people. My account is Murdo#0949, but be warned that I don't generally accept friend requests from people I haven't already got to know.

GitHub

This one is fairly obvious; this whole website is hosted on open-source GitHub (link in the footer)! I'm on there as MurdoMaclachlan.

Most of my activity on GitHub involves the development of some Python projects related to the Transcribers of Reddit, as well as the maintenance of a collection of transcription templates for common memes and reposts.

LinuxQuestions

LinuxQuestions.org is a forum hub for Linux, unsurprisingly. My account name there is just murdo. You won't find much in the way of posts or comments from me; I mainly just lurk conversations in order to stay in the loop.

Nexus Mods

Although I rarely use it at the moment, I consider my Nexus account, MBMaclachlan, to be active, as I intend to return to modmaking there when I have time, and I still maintain and provide supports for those mods I've published.

My most popular mod on Nexus is a Witcher 3 mod, No Dismemberments or Finishers, which disables some of the more gratuitously violent aspects of the game, but I'm personally far more invested in my Grimrock imports, bringing enemies from the first game into the second one for use in custom dungeons.

Reddit

My most active account besides Discord, I'm on Reddit as MurdoMaclachlan, and mainly use my account for transcribing.

In my early days on Reddit, I would mainly post to r/worldbuilding, but since joining the Transcribers I've done little else. I don't post to r/worldbuilding these days, as I'm trying to focus fully on writing stories rather than getting carried away in world after world.

I do my best not to get into arguments about that with people, but inherently there will be people who see things they disagree with on that account, so stay away if you disagree with me politically and are easily triggered.
